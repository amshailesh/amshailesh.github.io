About us

Our Company

We introduce ourselves as one of the prime Interior Decorators in Thane. We are into this creative field for the last 15 years. We take on any interior job as a new challenge and specialize in low cost, graceful & functional concepts which are speedy & time bound.The clients trust us by offering new responsibilities, and we bestow the trust reposed in us by converting their dreams into reality. Homeecraft believes in teaming up with our clients and supports their dreams with our vision and skill-set. This facilitates us to outline innovative and responsive interior concepts within the framework of time and money, absolutely exclusive for each client who plays a critical role in shaping the design of their projects. They aren't surprised by our designs; rather they see it evolve throughout the designing process. We strive to meet customer expectations.
