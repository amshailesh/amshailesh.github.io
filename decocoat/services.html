Asset protection is not at least a question of the right coating system. DCPL systems are applied in the 2 K spraying process and guarantee reliable surface protection against abrasion, impact, corrosion, aggressive liquids, sunlight and chemicals. Our coating systems can be applied under the most difficult climatic conditions (heat, cold, high humidity) and form a resilient and flexible surface.
