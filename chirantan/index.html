About our trust

Chirantan sanmitra trust is organised for the welfare of living beings .
It has aim to work for the betterment of needy weaker sections of indian society and assure their growth in all sectors like health , education, shelter and try to establish healthy society to form a strong nation.
Besides serving human trust is bound to take care of animals specially goraksha (protecting cow)and gopalan (bringing up cow).

Our Aim

The mission

1. To help weaker sections such as people below poverty,tribals, ill and incompetent to ensure their alround growth.

2. Aurvedic health research center to create awareness regarding health.

3. Arrangement to perform wedding's in group for tribals and other weaker sections of society.

4. To facilate organic food consumption by making them available for reasonable prices in turn enhance bio food farming.

5. Meditation center's to focus on mental and physical health.

6. Rain harvesting and other useful projects to restore our lovely planet.

7. Skill development in rural areas.

8. 'Annachatra' that is availability of fresh and healthy food for needy people around the clock.

9. To perform yagna -yag for welfare of universe.

10. All other duties towards betterment of society.
