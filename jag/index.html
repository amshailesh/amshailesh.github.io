Corrosion Prevention

Eliminate product damage caused by moisture ingress with our barrier foil laminate range. With an inner layer of aluminium, barrier foils will protect your products from corrosion and moisture damage. Our Aluminium Barrier Laminates can be supplied as custom-made 3D Bags, Flat Bags or Roll Stock. We offer a wide range of custom bag formats in a variety of laminate grades including laminates which are approved to worldwide defence and military standards and anti-static laminates.

Dry Product Protection

Barrier Foils provide total climatic protection to any dry product which may deteriorate due to attack from water vapour, oxygen and other gases, UV light, odour transfer in and out and temperature extremes. Our Aluminium Barrier Foil Bags and Liners can be customised to fit any sized Gaylord Box, Drum/Pail, Octabin and F.I.B.C. We also manufacture 25kg sacks, 20′ and 40′ ISO Container Liners, Large Flat/Envelope Style Barrier Bags and Continuous Tubing in lay-flat or gusseted formats.
